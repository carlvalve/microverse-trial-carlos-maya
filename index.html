Here's a timeline of Steve Job's life:

  • 1946 — Steve Jobs was born in San Francisco on February 24, 1955
  • 1954 — He was the chairman, chief executive officer (CEO), and co-founder of Apple Inc.
  • 1964 — Jobs is widely recognized as a pioneer of the microcomputer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s, along with Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak.
  • 1967 — Jobs and Wozniak co-founded Apple in 1976 to sell Wozniak's Apple I personal computer.
